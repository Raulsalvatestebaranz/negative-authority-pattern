# Architecture Decision Record (ADR)

This template has no authority. It does not create rules, permissions, or obligations and may not be cited to justify action or enforcement.

## Context

Describe the situation that required a decision.
Focus on constraints, pressures, and observed problems.
Avoid hindsight and justification language.

---

## Decision

State the decision that was made.
Be precise and declarative.

---

## Rationale

Explain why this decision was chosen over alternatives.
Reference trade-offs explicitly.
Tie reasoning back to constitutional invariants where relevant.

---

## Consequences

Describe what this decision enables and what it constrains.
Include both positive and negative consequences.

---

## Alternatives considered

List meaningful alternatives that were considered and why they were not chosen.
If no alternatives were considered, state why.

---

## Reversibility

Describe how this decision could be reversed, replaced, or retired.
If reversal is costly or difficult, state that explicitly.

---

Optional.
Include clarifications that future readers may need to interpret this record accurately.
